Teachers implementing this module can order a study box containing pinned dead insects of different groups of Hymenoptera that may be captured in pan traps to aid identification. The study box also contains QR codes leading to more information that students can access with their smartphones, and a hologram box to view 3 D images of insects on their smartphones. To order a study box contact: John Heraty.
